What's Included

A complete review of what's working

Every audit covers six key areas that directly affect whether your website converts visitors into enquiries, bookings and memberships.

Design & Branding

Does your site look like a premium fitness brand or a template from 2018? We review layout, typography, colour use and visual consistency to see if your brand builds trust at first glance.

User Experience

Can a potential client land on your site and immediately understand what you offer, who it's for and what to do next? We check navigation, structure and clarity so nothing gets in the way of a booking.

Conversion Review

Traffic means nothing without conversions. We look at your calls to action, lead capture, offer positioning and booking flow to find where potential clients are dropping off.

Mobile Experience

Most of your audience will visit on their phone. We test how your site looks, loads and functions on mobile — because a poor mobile experience means lost enquiries.

Are you showing up when someone searches for a personal trainer or gym in your area? We review your title tags, meta descriptions, headings and basic on-page structure.

Speed & Technical

Slow sites lose visitors. We check load times, image sizes, core web vitals and technical issues that could be silently hurting your search rankings and user experience.

The Problem

Why fitness businesses lose leads
before a conversation even starts

Your website is often the first impression someone has of your business. If it doesn't build trust quickly, they'll leave — and book with someone else.

01

Confusing offers

If a visitor can't immediately understand what you offer, who it's for and how to get started, they won't stick around to figure it out. Clarity converts.

02

Low trust signals

No testimonials, no clear branding, no professional photography. Without trust, visitors won't hand over their email — let alone book a consultation.

03

Poor structure

Pages that don't guide the visitor towards an action. No clear path from landing on the site to enquiring, booking or signing up. Every extra click is a lost lead.

04

Weak mobile experience

Over 70% of your visitors are on their phone. If your site is slow, hard to read or difficult to navigate on mobile, you're turning away the majority of your traffic.

05

Outdated visuals

A site that looks like it hasn't been updated in years tells potential clients that your business might be the same. Premium services need premium presentation.

06

No clear next step

Every page should have a clear call to action. If your visitor finishes reading and doesn't know what to do next, that's a missed opportunity for a booking or enquiry.
